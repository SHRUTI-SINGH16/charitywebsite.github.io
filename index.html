Why Donate?

No one should worry about how to get to treatment.

The world-wide pandemic has caused devastating interruptions in ongoing cancer treatment for scores of cancer patients. The pandemic has posed a high risk for these patients with compromised immunity, causing upstaging of disease. The Sparks Foundation Charity has set up a special fund to ensure continuous support to cancer patients during the catastrophic COVID-19 outbreak, especially for patients who are battling the two big Cs, Cancer and Covid-19, together. We continue to network with hospitals and treatment centers to provide them with desperately needed financial and medical support.

Imagine a world free from cancer. Help make it a reality.
